# FlowCredit

**FlowCredit** is a decentralized revenue-based lending protocol built on the Creditcoin blockchain. It enables entrepreneurs and businesses to access capital without collateral by sharing a percentage of their future revenue with lenders until a predefined repayment cap is reached.

Traditional lending systems rely heavily on collateral, lengthy approval processes, and opaque credit scoring models. This excludes many entrepreneurs, especially in emerging markets, from accessing the capital they need to grow their businesses.

FlowCredit introduces a transparent, on-chain financing model where lenders and borrowers align incentives through revenue sharing rather than collateral or equity dilution.

---

# The Problem

Traditional lending markets are built around collateralized debt and strict credit evaluations.

Banks and financial institutions typically require:

• Real estate or physical collateral
• Personal guarantees
• Extensive credit history
• Lengthy approval processes

For entrepreneurs in emerging markets, these requirements create a massive barrier to accessing capital.

Even profitable businesses may be unable to secure loans simply because they lack traditional collateral or formal credit records.

As a result, many founders must either give up equity to investors or abandon growth opportunities entirely.

FlowCredit addresses this gap by introducing a decentralized revenue-based lending system that removes collateral requirements and aligns repayment with real business performance.

---

# The Solution

FlowCredit allows borrowers to raise capital by agreeing to share a percentage of their future revenue until a predetermined repayment cap is reached.

Instead of fixed monthly payments, repayments scale dynamically with the success of the borrower’s business.

This model benefits both parties:

Borrowers gain access to capital without collateral or equity dilution.

Lenders gain exposure to business growth while maintaining transparent and enforceable loan terms.

All loan agreements are stored and enforced through immutable smart contracts on the Creditcoin blockchain.

---

# How FlowCredit Works

### 1. Connect Wallet

Users begin by connecting their Web3 wallet to the FlowCredit application.

This allows the application to interact with the Creditcoin network and enables secure blockchain transactions.

---

### 2. Create Loan Request

Borrowers create a loan request by defining several key parameters:

• Loan amount
• Revenue share percentage
• Repayment cap

Example:

Loan Amount: 1000 CTC
Revenue Share: 5%
Repayment Cap: 1300 CTC

This means the borrower receives 1000 CTC and repays through revenue sharing until the total repayment reaches 1300 CTC.

The smart contract records these terms on-chain and publishes the loan request to the platform.

---

### 3. Browse Loans

Lenders can browse available loan requests through the FlowCredit interface.

Each loan listing displays:

• Borrower address
• Loan amount
• Funding status
• Loan terms

This transparency allows lenders to evaluate opportunities before committing capital.

---

### 4. Fund Loan

When a lender chooses to fund a loan, they simply click the fund button and confirm the transaction through their wallet.

The smart contract transfers funds directly from the lender to the borrower and locks the loan terms permanently.

Once funded, the loan becomes active and cannot be modified.

---

### 5. Revenue-Based Repayment

Borrowers repay lenders through a percentage of business revenue rather than fixed monthly payments.

Repayments continue until the total amount paid reaches the predefined repayment cap.

This ensures that:

• Borrowers never overpay
• Repayment scales with business performance
• Lenders benefit from borrower success

Once the repayment cap is reached, the smart contract automatically stops accepting payments and closes the loan.

---

# Smart Contract Guarantees

FlowCredit smart contracts enforce several critical protections:

**Immutable Loan Terms**
Loan parameters cannot be altered once the loan is funded.

**Repayment Cap Enforcement**
Borrowers cannot be charged beyond the predefined repayment cap.

**Non-Custodial Architecture**
Funds are controlled by smart contracts rather than centralized intermediaries.

**Transparent On-Chain Records**
All loan activity is publicly verifiable on the blockchain.

# Why Creditcoin

Creditcoin is purpose-built for recording and verifying real-world credit transactions.

This makes it an ideal infrastructure layer for decentralized lending systems like FlowCredit.

Key advantages include:

• Transparent credit transaction records
• Fast finality and low transaction fees
• Cross-border lending infrastructure
• Decentralized validator network
• Designed for emerging market financial inclusion

FlowCredit leverages Creditcoin’s architecture to create a more accessible and transparent lending ecosystem.
