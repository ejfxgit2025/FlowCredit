How Revenue-Based Lending Works

Step 1 — Borrower Creates Loan Contract

A business deploys a revenue-based loan agreement defining: requested funding, revenue share percentage, repayment cap, loan duration. These terms are encoded into a smart contract.

Step 2 — Lenders Fund the Loan

Investors browse active loan requests and fund them using their Web3 wallet. Funds are locked inside an on-chain escrow contract.

Step 3 — Smart Contract Distributes Revenue

As the borrower generates revenue, payments are submitted on-chain and automatically distributed between lenders according to the revenue share agreement.

FlowCredit Blockchain Architecture

FlowCredit is built on the Creditcoin network to enable transparent and verifiable lending relationships between borrowers and lenders.

The architecture consists of:

  • → Borrower
  • → Revenue Loan Smart Contract
  • → Escrow Contract
  • → Revenue Payment Reports
  • → Automatic Distribution to Lenders

All agreements are recorded on-chain and cannot be modified after deployment.
